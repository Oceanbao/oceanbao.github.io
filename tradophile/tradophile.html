RECALL

  • 1D-50MA, a key/risky case, 1) always check bigger trend, possibly past 1D-DIV, then best wait till KD-X (<20), MACD-X

  • 1D-100MA is awkward level, because it means a definite below-MACD-0 fall, heading to MACD-X, watch BOLL touch + KD<20 (if a 1W-DIV, wait long time and watch 1W-KD)

  • MACD > MA > BOLL

  • 1W-MA lines more important, touch more reliable and possible than 1D ones, W-50MA key trend line

  • 1W KD is key guide if all else fails

KEY

  • Long 1W rally spans 2-3 peaks (forming a convex cone) with 50KD rebounds (1D BOLL touch) (i.e. 2-3 1D-MACD-DIV sets, each worth doing short term 4H trades, 'safe on first, then watch out after the second exit') (case: pre-2000 2 sets)

  • 1D-50MA-MACD-0 line: it seems all MACD underwater X (bar big ones, which? DIG) are safe entries! SO: key to find exceptions, DIV and big-dive cases! i.e. 1W-50MA cases?

    • NOT big rally: because those are 1D-20MA pullbacks, > MACD-0 (play: 1D-DIV + 4H-MACD-X)

    • 1D-100/200MA, MACD-DIV case

    • sideway channel: BOLL spikes, quick V

    • cut 1D-200MA, definte DIV case, watch 1W BOLL hit

    • 1W-20MA hit = 1D BOLL (100MA or 200MA), UPTREND, quick V

  • 4H KD-X: turns out very reliable signal - notwithstanding how much of a bounce, it is a very reliable BUT 4H-KD stalls during D jittery or DOWNTREND (W-KD downhill)

    • Normal entry: 15Min-MACD-X precisely at 4H-KD-X

    • Stalling: use 4H-MACD-X instead

  • D-KD-<20: very likely V bounce (use 4H-MACD-X entry)

  • D-20-50MA pullbacks (UPTREND): normal pattern is 20MA tick (V at 4H-MACD) between 2 peaks of a D-DIV, then a 50MA tick (4H-MACD-DIV) after D-DIV; NOTE: the 50MA would need to be a touch, otherwise it could well fall through D-MACD-0 to become BOLL fall.
